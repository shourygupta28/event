GAME-PLAY

Below are the Game Play and instructions for participants to join and play the game:

BIDDING

  • There will be a limited number of companies for bidding.
  • Every participant will be provided with an initial capital of 1,50,000 E-Coins.
  • Bidding will take place in 4 rounds.
  • Ones with the highest bid will get the company.
  • A participant can’t be the highest bidder of two companies at the same time in a particular round.
  • Once a participant has been allotted a company he/she can’t bid in the following rounds.
  • There will be a break of 10 minutes after every round.
  • The aim of participants is to get a company.

If there are any technical issues feel free to mail us at tuedcell@gmail.com No forget password request will be handled after 21st August!

TRADING

  • News Flashes from all around the world will be made in between the game which may or may not affect participant’s company.
  • E-coins (equal to 1000 X Multiplying factor of company X Percentage of share) will be added in your accounts every hour.
  • Participants will get a profit of 5 hours at the end of trading as per the previous formula.
  • All the trades must be closed by 7pm else the participants will not get the credit for them.
  • Participants should make sure that their E-coins aren’t below 0 else they’ll face losses in the following rounds.
  • Participants won’t get E-coins for the percentage of shares of their companies in trade.
  • Participants will be disqualified if our system detects any illegal trade or insider trading.
  • Participants with the highest E-coins will win.

OVERVIEW

  • Participants can find the multiplying factor with the companies by clicking on the Companies tab on the navigation bar.
  • Participants can find the highest bid and the company name under the Bid tab on the navigation bar.
  • Companies available for trade with the lowest bid can be found by clicking on the Trading tab on the navigation bar.
  • Participants can put their companies for trade by going to My Companies which comes in the dropdown box of Hi Username tab.
  • Participants can find their trades in My Trades which lies in the dropdown box of Hi Username tab.
  • Participants can find their bids placed on trades in My Bids which lies in the dropdown box of Hi Username tab.
